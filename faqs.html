Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the College Help Desk System?

The College Help Desk System is a platform designed to assist students and staff with various issues, providing a structured way to report and resolve problems.

2. How do I submit a ticket?

You can submit a ticket by logging into the help desk system and navigating to the "Submit Ticket" section. Fill out the required fields and describe your issue.

3. What types of issues can I report?

You can report issues related to IT support, academic inquiries, administrative tasks, facilities management, and more.

4. How will I know the status of my ticket?

You will receive email notifications regarding updates to your ticket. You can also check the status by logging into your account on the help desk system.

5. Who should I contact for urgent issues?

For urgent matters, please contact the appropriate department directly via phone or email. The help desk is best suited for non-urgent issues.

6. What if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, you can use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it.
